The Godmother, Enya

Enya, the godmother of RMS Ardent, is a renowned Irish singer, songwriter, and musician known for her ethereal sound and unique blend of Celtic, folk, and new age music. Born Eithne Pádraigín Ní Bhraonáin on May 17, 1961, in County Donegal, Ireland, Enya's career spans decades, with her debut album "The Celts" gaining international acclaim. Her signature style, featuring multi-layered vocals and atmospheric melodies, has earned her Grammy Awards and a global fanbase. Enya's music has touched millions, and her involvement as the godmother of SS Ardent symbolizes her cultural influence and connection to the sea.

She will sing live with her newest song "This Green Heaven" debuting on Christening day!
